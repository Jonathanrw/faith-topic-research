# Night Anxiety Prayer Guide

*Find quiet for your mind and peace for your night — one short prayer at a time.*

A short, faith-centered PDF designed to help calm anxious thoughts at night with Scripture, simple prayers, and gentle reflection prompts. Free to download and easy to use when fear or panic comes after dark.

## What you'll get
- Short, calming prayers to invite God's presence in the moment
- Carefully chosen Scriptures that soothe worry and strengthen faith
- Reflection prompts to help you name fears and pray them honestly
- Practical, gentle steps to create a more peaceful bedtime routine

Format: PDF — free to download and read on any device.

## Who this is for
This guide is for Christian adults who struggle with fear, panic, or late-night anxiety and want a faith-based, compassionate way to quiet racing thoughts and rest in God's care. It’s simple enough to use in the middle of the night and grounded in Scripture and prayer.

## How to use it
Read a short prayer and a verse, take a minute with a reflection prompt, and breathe slowly as you invite God's presence. Use the guide whenever anxious thoughts surface — even at 2 a.m.
