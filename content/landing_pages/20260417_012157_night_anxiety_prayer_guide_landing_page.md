# Night Anxiety Prayer Guide

A short, gentle faith-based PDF to help calm anxious thoughts at night with prayer and Scripture. Designed for quiet moments when fear or panic creep in and you need a peaceful way to settle your mind.

## A simple, comforting resource for sleepless nights
When the house is quiet and worries grow louder, a few focused prayers and a Scripture can bring perspective and peace. This guide gives you short prayers, calming passages, and reflection prompts you can use right away—no long reads, just practical spiritual support.

## What’s inside
- Carefully chosen Scriptures to read when anxiety rises
- Short, calming prayers you can say aloud or silently
- Reflection prompts to re-center your heart and mind
- Easy-to-use layout for late-night reading (PDF format)

## Benefits
- Quiet racing thoughts and anchor your mind in God’s truth
- Replace fear-driven loops with short, faith-filled prayers
- Restore perspective and calm in minutes, even during panic
- Build a simple nightly practice that supports better rest

## Who this is for
This guide is for Christian adults who struggle with fear, panic, or late-night anxiety and want faith-based, practical tools to find calm. It’s gentle, non-technical, and meant for anyone who needs a short, trusted way to pray and remember Scripture when worry comes.

## How to use it
Download the PDF, keep it on your phone or bedside, and turn to it when anxiety starts at night. Read a Scripture, speak a short prayer, and use the reflection prompts to breathe and re-center on God’s presence.
